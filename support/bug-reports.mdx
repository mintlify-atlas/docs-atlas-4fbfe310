---
title: "Report a bug"
description: "Help improve CloudBook by submitting a bug report. No account required."
---

If you find something that isn't working as expected, the bug report form lets you send details directly to the CloudBook team. Reports are reviewed manually, and if you provide your email address the team may follow up to gather more information.

<Note>
  The bug report form is available to all users — you don't need a CloudBook account to submit a report.
</Note>

## How to submit a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the bug report form">
    Go to [list-app.vercel.app/bug-reports](https://list-app.vercel.app/bug-reports).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the bug">
    In the **Describe the bug** field, explain what went wrong. Include what you were doing, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened.
  </Step>
  <Step title="List the reproduction steps">
    In **Steps to reproduce**, write a numbered list of actions that cause the bug to appear. For example:

    1. Open the app.
    2. Create a new note.
    3. Press save.
    4. The app closes unexpectedly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your device and app version">
    Choose the **device** (Android or Windows) and the **app version** where you encountered the bug.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the date">
    Set the **date** when the bug occurred.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add optional details (recommended)">
    Optionally, provide your **email address** for follow-up and a **screenshot URL** (for example, an Imgur link) to help the team reproduce the issue faster.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Send report**. You'll see a confirmation message when your report is received.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form fields

### Required fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Bug description | A clear explanation of the problem, including what you expected vs. what happened. |
| Reproduction steps | Numbered steps that reliably reproduce the bug. |
| Device | The platform where the bug occurred: Android or Windows. |
| App version | The version of CloudBook where the issue appeared. |
| Date | The date when you first encountered the bug. |

### Optional fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Email | Your contact address. The CloudBook team may reach out if they need more details. |
| Screenshot URL | A direct link to an image of the bug (e.g., an Imgur URL). |

<Tip>
  The more detail you include, the faster the team can identify and fix the issue. Exact reproduction steps and a screenshot are especially helpful.
</Tip>
