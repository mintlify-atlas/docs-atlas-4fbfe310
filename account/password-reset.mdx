---
title: "Password reset"
description: "Reset a forgotten password or change your current password while logged in."
---

CloudBook gives you two ways to update your password: a reset flow for when you're locked out, and an in-app flow for when you're already logged in.

<Tip>
  Use a password that is at least 8 characters long and mixes letters, numbers, and symbols. CloudBook requires a minimum of 6 characters, but a longer password is harder to guess.
</Tip>

## Reset a forgotten password

Use this flow when you can't log in because you've forgotten your password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Open [https://list-app.vercel.app](https://list-app.vercel.app) and click **Forgot password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address associated with your CloudBook account and submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    CloudBook sends a password reset email containing a **Change password** link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the reset link">
    Click **Change password** in the email. CloudBook opens a password reset form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new password">
    Type your new password in the **New password** field, then re-enter it in the **Confirm password** field. Your password must be at least 6 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and log in">
    Click **Change password**. Once the reset succeeds, go back to the login page and sign in with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The password reset link is valid for **2 hours**. If you don't click it in time, request a new reset link by going through the forgot-password flow again.
</Warning>

## Change your password while logged in

If you know your current password and want to update it, you can do so from your account settings without going through the email flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Log in to CloudBook and navigate to **Account settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current password">
    Type your existing password in the **Current password** field to confirm your identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new password">
    Type the new password you want to use in the **New password** field. Your new password must be different from your current one — CloudBook will reject the change if they match.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the update. Your password is hashed with bcrypt before being stored.
  </Step>
</Steps>
