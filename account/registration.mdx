---
title: "Account registration"
description: "Create your CloudBook account and start taking encrypted notes."
---

Registering gives you a CloudBook account tied to your email address. Once you're signed up, your notes are encrypted and stored in the cloud so you can access them from any device.

## Register for CloudBook

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    Go to [https://list-app.vercel.app](https://list-app.vercel.app) and click **Sign up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Type your email address and choose a password, then click **Register**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    CloudBook sends a welcome email to the address you registered with. The email confirms your registration and includes a **Cancel registration** link in case you didn't sign up yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using CloudBook">
    Your account is created immediately. You are logged in and can start creating encrypted notes right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your password is hashed with bcrypt before it is stored. CloudBook never saves your password in plaintext — not in the database, not in logs, not anywhere.
</Note>

## If you didn't register

If you receive a welcome email but didn't sign up, someone may have entered your email address by mistake. You can remove the account before it's used.

<Warning>
  The **Cancel registration** link in the welcome email is valid for **24 hours** from the moment the account was created. After that window closes, the link expires and the account remains active. If you miss the window, contact CloudBook support to have the account removed.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the welcome email">
    Find the CloudBook welcome email in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel registration">
    Click the **Cancel registration** button in the email. CloudBook opens a confirmation page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Click **Yes, I'm sure** on the confirmation page. The account linked to your email is permanently deleted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
