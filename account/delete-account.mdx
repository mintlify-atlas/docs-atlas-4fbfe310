---
title: "Delete account"
description: "Permanently delete your CloudBook account and all of your notes."
---

Deleting your account removes your profile and every note you've created. This action cannot be reversed.

<Warning>
  Account deletion is **permanent**. All of your notes are erased along with your account. There is no way to recover your data after deletion. Make sure you export any notes you want to keep before proceeding.
</Warning>

## Delete your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Log in to CloudBook and navigate to **Account settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select delete account">
    Scroll to the danger zone section and click **Delete account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your password">
    Type your current password to confirm that you are the account owner. CloudBook requires your password before proceeding — this step prevents accidental or unauthorized deletion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Click **Confirm** to permanently delete your account. CloudBook immediately removes your account and all associated notes from its servers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After your account is deleted, your login session is invalidated. Any existing token is no longer accepted — attempting to use it returns an unauthorized error. You will need to create a new account if you want to use CloudBook again.
