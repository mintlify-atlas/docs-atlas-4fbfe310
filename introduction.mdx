CloudBook is a note-taking platform built around one principle: your notes are yours alone. Every note you create is AES-encrypted on the server before being stored in the cloud, and your password is never stored in readable form. Whether you're on the web or using the mobile app, CloudBook keeps your information private and always available.

## Why CloudBook?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Server-side AES encryption" icon="lock">
    Notes are encrypted with AES on the server before being written to the database. Plaintext content is never persisted.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-platform" icon="devices">
    Access your notes on the web, Android, and Windows from a single account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Simple & fast" icon="bolt">
    A distraction-free editor with rich text formatting and instant export to Markdown, TXT, or HTML.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure account recovery" icon="shield-halved">
    Token-based password reset and registration cancellation protect your account at every step.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Register with your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email that lets you cancel the registration within 24 hours if you didn't initiate it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your first note">
    Use the in-browser editor or the mobile app to create a note. Give it a title, write your content, and save — CloudBook encrypts it automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access from anywhere">
    Log in from any browser or the mobile app with your credentials. Your encrypted notes sync instantly across all your devices.
  </Step>
</Steps>
